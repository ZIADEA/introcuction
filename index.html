About

I am an engineering student in Artificial Intelligence and Data Technology for Industrial Systems at ENSAM Meknès. I am interested in reinforcement learning, mobile robotics, computer vision and applied machine learning for industrial environments.

My work combines practical projects (robot navigation, simulation, dashboards, web applications) with research-oriented topics such as RL for control, generative design, and data-driven decision making in industrial systems. I enjoy turning complex technical ideas into clear tools, demos and teaching material.
